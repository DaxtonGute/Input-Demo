Form Editor

Create and edit your forms here. To view your form, click 'view'. Keep in mind, no changes will be reflected there until your changes are finalised by pressing 'published'. Otherwise you will just see your previous form for reference. Also, don't forget radio groups must come before radio buttons. Seems simplistic but you'd be surprised :).

Google Forms!

View your previous/current form here. To edit your form, click 'edit'. That will give you various options to construct a form that, once published, can be tested and saved here. If you click 'save' all of your previous answers are saved. This way even after leaving and returning you need only to press 'Autofill' to view your questions as well as their answers. When you are all finished. Feel free to copy the form section of the code and paste it to your website for an easy customizable form
